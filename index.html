About DJ Msholozi

DJ Msholozi is a passionate and energetic performer known for igniting dancefloors across South Africa. With a signature blend of Afro-house, Amapiano, and modern beats, Msholozi has become a crowd favorite, bringing unforgettable vibes to every event.

He began his career in 2017, and after 8 years in the music scene, he continues to grow and evolve. His journey has taken him to stages in Bloemfontein (Free State), Potchefstroom and Vryburg (North West), as well as Pella, Namaqualand, Kimberley, and Upington (Northern Cape). He has also made appearances on national radio, including 2000 (SABC Studios).

DJ Msholozi is the visionary behind some of the most talked-about events in the country. His most successful and iconic productions include the annual Cuff Your Chick Larnie (CYCL) hosted by him and his dedicated team.
